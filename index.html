❓ Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QRGen Pro and QR codes.

Is QRGen Pro really free?

Yes! QRGen Pro is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, no watermarks, and no registration required. You can generate unlimited QR codes without any cost.

Do the QR codes expire?

No, the QR codes you generate are static and will work forever. Since we do not store your data on our servers, the QR codes are permanent and independent of our service.

What QR code types are supported?

We support 7 QR code types: URL, Plain Text, WiFi Network, vCard (Contact), Email, Phone Number, and SMS. Each type is optimized for its specific use case.

Can I use the QR codes commercially?

Absolutely! All QR codes generated with QRGen Pro can be used for both personal and commercial purposes without any restrictions or attribution requirements.

What download formats are available?

You can download your QR codes in PNG (for digital use), SVG (for vector editing), and PDF (for print materials). All formats are high-resolution and ready to use.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes! We do not store, log, or track any of the data you enter. All QR codes are generated client-side using public APIs. Your information never touches our servers.

What is error correction and which level should I choose?

Error correction allows QR codes to be readable even if partially damaged. Low (7%) is fine for clean digital use. Medium (15%) is recommended for most cases. High (30%) is best for print materials that may get dirty or damaged.

Can I customize the colors of my QR code?

Yes! You can customize both the foreground (QR pattern) and background colors. Just ensure there is sufficient contrast between them for optimal scanning.

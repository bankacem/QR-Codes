Designing Beautiful QR Codes That People Want to Scan

Mar 10, 2026 5 min read

Gone are the days when QR codes had to be boring black-and-white boxes. With modern design tools and generators like QRGen Pro, you can create visually stunning QR codes that complement your brand and increase engagement.

The Importance of Aesthetics

A well-designed QR code can increase scan rates by up to 30%. When a QR code looks like a part of the design rather than an afterthought, users are more likely to trust it and interact with it. Custom colors, logos, and unique frames can all contribute to a better user experience.

Design Best Practices

  • Contrast is Key: Always maintain a high contrast between the foreground and background colors. If the colors are too similar, scanners will struggle to read the code.
  • Size Matters: Don't make your QR code too small. Ensure it's large enough to be scanned easily from a reasonable distance.
  • Add a Call to Action: Surround your QR code with a clear CTA like "Scan to Win" or "View Menu" to let users know what to expect.
  • Test, Test, Test: Always test your custom QR code with multiple devices and scanning apps to ensure it's still functional.

Advanced Customization

Consider using your brand's primary color for the QR pattern. You can also experiment with rounded corners or custom eye shapes to give it a unique look. Just remember to keep functionality at the forefront of your design decisions.

By following these simple tips, you can turn a functional tool into a beautiful design element that drives results for your brand.
